The Skeletal System

Overview

The skeletal system provides the framework for our body, protects vital organs, and enables movement in conjunction with muscles.

Learning Objectives:
  • Understand bone structure and composition
  • Identify major bones in the human body
  • Learn about different types of joints
  • Understand the role of the skeletal system

1. Bone Structure

  • Compact Bone: Dense, strong outer layer
  • Spongy Bone: Lighter, honeycomb interior
  • Bone Marrow: Blood cell production site
